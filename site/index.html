Vision

In the future envisioned by the quality movement, small or large communities of people are highly self-reliant. Trades are restored. Shoe makers, bakers, tool makers, builders. Real jobs which matter. Bullshit jobs are gone. Automated away using technology. There are no bankers or real-estate agents. The middle-man is cut out, and builders are directly connected to buyers.

Manifesto

The quality movement is against the massive concentration of wealth.

Since wealth is so massively centralised at the moment by massive brands the quality of goods is not commensurate price of those goods

the quality movement is about realigning the value of goods with the price of those goods

It's about cutting through hype and marketing.

Quality over Brands

I really distrust brands. The original idea had merit, but they have now devolved into simple deception, almost true is that: Brand = Lie.

I like the idea of actual quality. Not someone trying to tell me that their product has quality. Measurable, factual quality.

The concept that a brand has to convince you to trust a company’s product or service just doesn’t seem useful any more.

In an era where we can use pervasive sensors, transparency and immediate communication there’s no reason for an intermediary. I can, or should soon be able to, find out for myself exactly the quality of the options available and select one based on price and quality metrics.
